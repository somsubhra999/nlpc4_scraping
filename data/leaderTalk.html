Welcome

Welcome, AIM associates, to our first ever AIM Chronicles! You might be wondering, “So what are these Chronicles anyway?” The AIM Chronicles are an invitation for you to get to know your leaders better. Coming into 2016 we blogged about changing our Business Unit’s name to Analytics and Information Management, AIM (spoken as the word “aim” and not initials). We also committed to sharing with you the organizational changes that would come along with that, however, we didn’t think you’d appreciate a long e-mail with a lot of pictures and facts about your leaders that you can easily look up on LinkedIn. So…we envisioned something totally radical. A leadership experience that tells you virtually nothing about their credentials to be leaders and as much as possible about who they are as people.

Through answering seven simple questions, and posting short videos, our hope is that you not only get to know, but get inspired by your leaders. You may also have some questions about some of the new titles that you are seeing in the Chronicles so we wanted to take a brief moment to clarify that as well:

  • TSG – Technology Solutions Group – This is the part of our business that will largely focus on everything that the erstwhile EIM was great at!
  • BSG – Business Solutions Group – This is where you will find our core/advanced analytics team. However, it is the responsibility of all AIM associates to learn analytics regardless of if you are a part of TSG or BSG.
  • AIM Head – By region, these are the overall leaders for the respective AIM verticals. Within each AIM vertical there will be a distinct TSG and BSG Group leader that rolls into the AIM Overall Head.
  • H2 – Horizon 2, and in the context of the Chronicles you will likely see this used to identify a number of our service lines within AIM including EDM, EIA and EPM just to name a few.

With that, we truly hope you enjoy the AIM Chronicles and do look forward to hearing your feedback! Please feel free to drop a note to Radha and I to let us know what you think. We also hope that the Chronicles inspire you to reach out to your leaders and to introduce yourself! All the best and enjoy!
